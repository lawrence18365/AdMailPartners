Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

What exactly does 'Category Exclusivity' mean for my business?

It means you GET to be the only business of your specific type within your chosen 5,000-home distribution group. If you're a dentist, no other dentist will appear in that mailing. If you run a landscaping company, yours will be the sole landscaping ad. This gives you a powerful advantage, ensuring that when a homeowner needs your service and sees our mailer, your business is the only option presented from us, dramatically increasing your chances of getting their call.

How can you offer advertising for such an affordable price per home?

You GET this incredible price through the power of shared distribution. By bringing together a small number of non-competing businesses targeting the same neighborhoods, we share the costs of printing and delivery. This efficiency allows us to pass significant savings onto you. You GET targeted reach without the massive overhead of solo direct mail, all while benefiting from category exclusivity which ensures you stand out, unlike other shared mail programs.
